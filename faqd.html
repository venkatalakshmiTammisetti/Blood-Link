Donor FAQs

Empowering you with answers — because every drop you give saves a life.

Am I eligible to donate blood?
You can donate if you're healthy, aged 18–65, and weigh over 50kg. A quick health check will be done before donation.
How frequently can I donate?
Whole blood can be donated every 3 months. Platelet donations can happen every 2 weeks.
What if I feel nervous or faint?
It's normal to feel anxious! Our team is here to support you. Relax, hydrate, and lie down if needed — you’ll be okay!
How do I prepare for donation day?
Drink water, eat a healthy meal (avoid oily food), and get enough sleep the night before.
What happens after I donate?
You'll be monitored for 10-15 minutes. Rest, hydrate, and enjoy a snack. You’ve just saved a life — thank you!
