patient FAQs

How can I request blood through this platform?
You can request blood by logging in as a hospital/clinic and filling out the blood request form with patient details and required blood type.
How quickly will I receive the required blood?
Our system notifies nearby donors instantly. Emergency requests are prioritized and fulfilled as soon as a donor is available.
Is the blood I receive tested and safe?
Absolutely. All donated blood is tested for infections and diseases to ensure it is completely safe for transfusion.
Can I request a specific blood type or component (like platelets)?
Yes. Our request form allows you to specify the blood group and component you need (e.g., whole blood, platelets, plasma).
How do I know my request is genuine and verified?
Our system includes a verification page where hospitals upload necessary documents. Only verified requests are published to protect donor trust.
